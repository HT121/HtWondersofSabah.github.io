The best hotels in Sabah consist of globally-renowned brands that have been providing excellent amenities and well-appointed guestrooms with unbeatable views for many years. Ranging from five-star resorts surrounded by immaculate beaches to centrally-located hotels, Sabah caters to opposite spectrums of budget levels and preferences.

Known for its prominent attractions such as Mount Kinabalu – Southeast Asia’s highest peak – and the Kinabalu National Park, Sabah offers more than just a picturesque landscape. This world-famous destination is primarily popular for its jungles as well as its seductive Sipadan coral reefs. The East Malaysian state is home to an interesting native population as well as the elusive orang utan, which can be found at the Sepilok Orang Utan Sanctuary. Sabah teems with a large variety of accommodation options, so deciding on the best one can be a daunting task.
